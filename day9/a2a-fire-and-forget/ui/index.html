A2A · push notifications

Fire & forget, over a webhook

The Orchestrator sends the Research Agent a non-blocking A2A message and gets its HTTP response back almost instantly. The agent keeps working after that response — there's no connection left open. When it's done, it POSTs the result to a webhook the orchestrator gave it. Watch the gap between the two.

📦 Delivered by webhook

A2A · tasks/get + tasks/cancel

The other half: poll it, or cancel it

The Long-Task Agent doesn't call anyone back. It's non-blocking the same way, but this time it's on us to check in with tasks/get — and we can change our mind mid-flight with tasks/cancel.
