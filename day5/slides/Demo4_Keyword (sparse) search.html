Keyword (sparse) search — hit one chunk, hit many, or miss entirely

Break the question into words, then ask each document one yes/no question: “do you contain this exact word?” Pick a knowledge base and a scenario, then press Run.

No embedding model anywhere in this demo. Keyword search never vectorizes the documents and never vectorizes the query — it only compares strings. That’s why it’s cheap and instant, and also why it can go blind (the red tab).
