Where does the text actually go? (and what does it cost?)

Click Embed: the app reads the PDF into chunks, then sends them to the model. API = pay per token; local = pay for hardware.

Local model: the text travels your intranet to an on-prem GPU cluster. No per-token bill — you paid for the hardware.
reading sample_drug_reference.pdf …
→ split into 14 chunks (size 800, overlap 150)
→ POST http://embeddings.internal:8080/embed (intranet, stays on your network)
← 200 OK · 14 × 1536-d vectors ($0 per token — your hardware)
vectors stored locally — ready to search
Nothing left your network and there is no per-token bill — but you bought the GPUs and you pay to power, cool, and run them. "Local" trades a metered cloud bill for capex + ops.
